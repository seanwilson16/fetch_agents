This agent, given any valid U.S. presidential election year from 1976 to 2020 and any valid U.S. state, will return that state's election results for that presidential election. Results include the winner of the state, the total number of votes cast in the state, a ranking of each candidate who received a vote in the state, and their respective vote totals.

Example Query:
"What were the results of the 2008 presidential election in Illinois?"

Example Response:
"Democrat candidate Barack H. Obama won Illinois in 2008. 5,522,371 people voted in total.

Here are the vote totals:

1. Barack H. Obama (Democrat): 3,419,348 votes
2. John Mccain (Republican): 2,031,179 votes
3. Ralph Nader (Independent): 30,948 votes
4. Bob Barr (Libertarian): 19,642 votes
5. Cynthia Mckinney (Green): 11,838 votes
6. Charles ""Chuck"" Baldwin (Constitution Party): 8,256 votes
7. Unknown Candidate (New): 1,149 votes
8. Unknown Candidate (Other): 11 votes"
